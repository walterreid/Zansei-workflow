# Small Business Marketing Insights

## Pain Points by Business Type

### Restaurants
- Limited marketing time (15hrs/week average)
- High customer churn (need retention)
- Seasonal revenue fluctuations
- Menu complexity affects messaging

### Retail Boutiques  
- Foot traffic dependency
- Inventory display challenges
- Local competition from chains
- Personal brand = business brand

## Budget Realities

### $0-500/month
Focus: Free channels (Google Business, organic social, email)
Avoid: Paid ads (insufficient budget for testing)

### $500-2000/month
Focus: Targeted local ads (Google Local, Facebook geo)
Test: 1-2 channels maximum
